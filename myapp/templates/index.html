Description of real estate

Categories of Real Estate: Residential: Properties used for living, like single-family homes, apartments, and condos.
Commercial: Buildings used for business purposes, such as offices, retail stores, and shopping centers.
Industrial: Properties used for manufacturing, warehousing, and distribution.
Raw Land: Undeveloped land, often used for future development.
Agricultural: Land used for farming, ranching, or forestry.
Special Use: Properties with unique purposes, like schools, hospitals, or places of worship.
